F.A.Q

  • How much does it cost to park?

    Self parking and valet parking is complimentary.

  • What time is Check-In?

    After 4 p.m. guests check in at the Boardwalk Inn's front desk.

  • What time is Check-Out?

    By 10 a.m. in Homes and Condos. By 11 a.m. in the Boardwalk Inn and The Village at Wild Dunes. A late departure fee may be assessed for departures after aforementioned times.

  • What address do I enter into GPS to get to Wild Dunes?

    Enter 1 Sundial Circle Isle of Palms, SC 29451. This will take you to the main Resort gate. Your arrival email will include the GPS address to your accommodation and driving directions from the main gate as well.

  • What Wi-Fi connectivity exists on the Resort?

    Wild Dunes offers complimentary Wi-Fi in all Boardwalk Inn guest rooms and all Village rooms and suites. As well, the Grand Pavilion, Palm Cove, Boardwalk Inn lobby area, and Village Plaza are all Wi-Fi hotspots. Because of the Resort’s remote location, some areas of the resort (including homes and condominiums) may have stronger signals than others.

  • What time can I arrive for late arrival?

    Late check-in is always available. However, if arriving after 8 p.m., please contact Wild Dunes at 843.886.6000 so that they may better plan for your arrival.
